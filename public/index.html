Directions (hide)

In the satellite-view image below, click on every tree you see that is right next to a street.

If there are no streets in the satellite image, or if you don't see any trees right next to a street, you may submit this HIT without marking any trees.

If you're not sure from the satellite view if something is a tree or not, click on it and look at the Street View panel. This should show you what the location you just clicked looks like from the street.

To mark a tree:

1. Left-click on the middle of the tree you see in the satellite image. A red map marker will appear.

2. Click the "Add Tree" button you see in the window that pops up. A tree icon will appear.

To remove a marker:

1. Left-click on the tree icon of the tree you would like to remove.

2. Click the "Remove" button you see in the window that pops up. The tree icon will disappear.

To submit this HIT:

When you are done marking trees, click the "Submit All Trees" button at the bottom of the page to submit the HIT.
